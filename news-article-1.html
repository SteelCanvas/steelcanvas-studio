Pocket Legion: From Concept to July 10th Release

June 10, 2025 • Steel Canvas Team • 6 min read

After months of development, we're thrilled to announce the official release strategy for Pocket Legion, our debut title that's redefining what mobile strategy games can be.

The Release Strategy

We're taking a measured approach with a soft release on July 10th, 2025 in the UK, Canada, and Australia. This isn't just about testing servers - it's about perfecting the player experience. These regions will get first access to our crowd-running strategy hybrid that combines the instant gratification of hypercasual games with genuine strategic depth.

What Makes Pocket Legion Different

Unlike typical mobile games that rely on predatory monetization, Pocket Legion respects your intelligence. It's wrapped in internet culture without talking down to players, featuring strategic crowd management where every decision matters. You're not just mindlessly tapping - you're planning, adapting, and outsmarting increasingly complex scenarios.

Global Release & Early Access

The worldwide release follows in September 2025, but Patreon supporters don't have to wait. Our supporters get early access to the global version, plus the satisfaction of knowing they helped fund a game that proves mobile gaming can be better.

This soft release approach lets us gather real player feedback, optimize performance, and ensure that when Pocket Legion goes global, it's not just functional - it's exceptional.

Support our development on Patreon and get early access to Pocket Legion's global release, plus exclusive behind-the-scenes content.
