Community Guidelines

To ensure our community remains welcoming and constructive for everyone, please follow these guidelines:

🤝 Be Respectful

Treat all community members with kindness and respect, regardless of their background or opinions.

💬 Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to Steel Canvas Studio, our games, and game development in general.

🚫 No Spam

Avoid posting repetitive content, excessive self-promotion, or irrelevant links.

🔒 Respect Privacy

Don't share personal information about yourself or others without permission.
