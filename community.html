Community Guidelines

To ensure our community remains welcoming and constructive for everyone, please follow these guidelines. Our community thrives on respect, creativity, and shared passion for gaming.

Be Respectful

Treat all community members with kindness and respect, regardless of their background or opinions. We're all here to share our love of gaming.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to Steel Canvas Studio, our games, and game development in general. Off-topic conversations should move to designated channels.

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Avoid posting repetitive content, excessive self-promotion, or irrelevant links. Quality contributions are always welcome.

Respect Privacy

Don't share personal information about yourself or others without permission. Keep our community safe and secure.

Constructive Feedback

Provide thoughtful, constructive feedback when discussing games or development. Help us improve by sharing your honest opinions respectfully.

Support Each Other

Help fellow community members with questions, celebrate achievements, and foster a positive environment where everyone can learn and grow together.
